Four Haiku

by Matsuo Basho

Spring:
A hill without a name
Veiled in morning mist.

The beginning of autumn:
Sea and emerald paddy
Both the same green.

The winds of autumn
Blow: yet still green
The chestnut husks.

A flash of lightning:
Into the gloom
Goes the hero's cry.

- Matsuo Basho, translated by Geoffrey Bownas and Anthony Thwaite
